What is covid19 ?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease,
caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

Symtoms

most common symptoms

  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

less common symptoms

  • aches and pains
  • sore thoat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivitis
  • headache
  • less of taste or smell
  • a rash on skin

serious symptoms

  • difficulty breathing
  • chest pain
  • loss of speech

Do & Don't

DO

  • Do wash hands with handwash
  • Do throw away used tissues
  • Do maintain social distancing
  • Do wear mask properly
  • Do maintain hygiene
  • Do get vaccination

DON'T

  • Do not visit hospital if you are unwell
  • Do not touch you mouth,nose in public place
  • Do not overstock medicines
  • Do not panic
  • Do not hug and handshake
